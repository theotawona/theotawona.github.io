About Me

A cross-disciplinary
engineer at heart

I'm a Mechanical Engineer by training, with a career that has deliberately crossed industries — from manufacturing and mineral processing to property finance and data science consulting. What connects it all is a systems mindset: the ability to see how the pieces fit, where the inefficiencies hide, and how data can drive better decisions.

On the financial side, I've built real capability from the ground up. As Finance Administrator and later Finance Manager, I took ownership of financial operations, designed payment processing systems, and built reporting pipelines that gave leadership clear, actionable visibility into performance. I understand P&L, cashflow, budget variance, and the financial mechanics that determine whether a business is actually healthy — not just on paper.

I founded Corporate Analytica to bring data science and automation into environments that need it most — combining engineering rigour with financial literacy to build solutions that are both technically sound and commercially meaningful. Currently deepening this through a Master's in Industrial Engineering with a specialisation in Data Science.
